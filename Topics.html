Purpose and Goals

The primary objective of the G20 is to promote international financial stability and sustainable economic growth. Member countries engage in discussions and coordinate policies on various topics such as macroeconomic policy, trade, investment, development, climate change, and global governance.

Annually, the G20 hosts a high-level ensemble of global leaders and financial experts. Together, they play a crucial tune of discussions spanning trade, investment, development, and climate change. This gathering orchestrates harmonious policies, nurturing worldwide economic stability and sustainable growth.

SDG-17

Global Partnerships for Sustainable Development is the focus of SDG 17, one of the Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations. To support the achievement of all SDGs, it aims to improve trade, boost technology transfer, and strengthen international cooperation. SDG 17 emphasises the value of cooperation between the public, private, and civil society sectors in addressing global challenges.

SDG 17 fosters global partnerships for sustainable development, encouraging collaboration among nations, businesses, and civil society to share knowledge, resources, and technology. It supports achieving all 16 other Sustainable Development Goals and tackling challenges like poverty, inequality, and climate change.

Countries forge alliances, allocate resources, share technological expertise, align policies, and exchange data to foster the progress of SDG 17. This involves enhancing global cooperation and coordination to ensure sustainable development, paving the way for a more inclusive and equitable world.

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of safeguarding computer systems, networks, and digital data from unauthorized access, attacks, damage, or theft. It involves implementing a combination of technical measures, protocols, and strategies to protect digital assets and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information in the digital realm.


At its essence, cybersecurity is a comprehensive practice aimed at safeguarding digital systems, networks, and data from a wide range of cyber threats, including unauthorized access, data breaches, malware attacks, and more. It involves implementing robust security measures, protocols, and technologies to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information in today's interconnected and technology-driven world..

Different Ways to Adopt Cyber Security

Use unique and complex passwords.

Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA).

Keep software and devices up to date.

Use secure Wi-Fi and networks.

Be cautious of suspicious links and emails.

Regularly back up important data.
